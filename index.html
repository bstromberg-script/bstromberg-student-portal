Delinquent Student Accounts

Monthly past due notices are sent to students who have outstanding balances. If a balance reaches $3,500 or more and is 60 days past due, the account is considered delinquent. Students will receive four notices. After the final notice, if unresolved, the school will determine withdrawal from the program.

Financial Aid

Grants are available covering approximately 75% of tuition. This reduces student out-of-pocket costs to about $2,000 per semester.
